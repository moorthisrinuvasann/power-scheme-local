Executive Summary

Scheme 1 (All-Buck) offers the highest efficiency (90%+) and thermal performance at 85°C, ideal for the 23A V6 load, but is the most expensive ($56,700). Scheme 2 (Mixed Topology) reduces cost ($18,400) by using LDOs for low-current rails fed from the 3.3V Buck, but incurs efficiency penalties and thermal stress on LDOs at 85°C due to high dropout. Scheme 3 (Integrated Multi-Output) provides the best cost-performance balance ($12,650) by using the LTM4671 Quad Buck to consolidate four low-voltage rails, minimizing component count while maintaining high efficiency and meeting all voltage/current constraints without LDO thermal issues.
