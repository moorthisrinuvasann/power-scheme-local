Executive Summary

Scheme 1 (All-Buck) offers the highest efficiency (90%+) and best thermal performance at 85°C but has the highest cost ($30,200) and component count. Scheme 2 (Buck + LDO Hybrid) is the most cost-effective ($14,500) by using LDOs for low-current rails, but risks thermal failure at 85°C due to high dropout losses on LDOs fed from 3.3V/5V rails. Scheme 3 (Balanced) provides a middle ground ($18,200), using Buck converters for moderate loads to manage heat while using LDOs only for very low current rails, ensuring thermal safety with reasonable cost.
