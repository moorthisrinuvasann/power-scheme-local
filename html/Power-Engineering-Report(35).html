Executive Summary

Scheme 1 (All-Buck) offers the highest thermal efficiency at 85°C but incurs the highest BOM cost ($27,400) due to redundant high-current converters for low-power rails. Scheme 2 (Hybrid) is the most cost-effective ($14,440) by using LDOs for low-current rails, though it sacrifices efficiency and generates more heat in the LDOs. Scheme 3 (Multi-Output) provides the best balance ($15,200), leveraging the LTM4671 to reduce component count for mid-range rails while maintaining high efficiency for high-current loads and using LDOs for noise-sensitive outputs.
