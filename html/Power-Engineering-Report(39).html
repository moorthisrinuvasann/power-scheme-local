Executive Summary

Scheme 1 (All-Buck) offers the highest efficiency (90%+) and best thermal performance at 85C ambient by eliminating LDO dropout losses, but at the highest cost (26,800) due to over-specifying high-current µModules for low-current rails. Scheme 2 (Cost Optimized) significantly reduces cost (10,200) by using LDOs for low-current rails (V3-V8), fed from V2 (5V) or V4 (1.8V), but introduces thermal risks if LDOs dissipate too much heat (e.g., 5V to 0.85V). Scheme 3 (Thermal Optimized) balances cost (11,500) and thermal performance by selecting specific low-dropout LDOs (TPS73701, ADP1763) and optimizing their input sources (e.g., feeding V8 from V1 instead of V2) to minimize power dissipation, while retaining Buck converters for high-current rails (V1, V2, V5).
