Executive Summary

Scheme 1 (All-Buck) offers the highest efficiency and best thermal performance at 85°C ambient, eliminating LDO dropout losses, but has the highest BOM cost ($22,200). Scheme 2 (Hybrid Buck+LDO) is the most cost-effective ($10,920) by using LDOs for low-current rails, though it sacrifices some efficiency and increases thermal stress on LDOs at high ambient temperatures. Scheme 3 (Multi-Output Consolidation) balances BOM complexity and performance ($14,600) by grouping low-voltage rails into multi-output modules, reducing component count while maintaining Buck efficiency across all rails.
