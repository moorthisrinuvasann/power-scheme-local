Executive Summary

Scheme 1 (All-Buck) offers the highest efficiency (90%+) and thermal robustness at 85°C but has the highest BOM cost ($12,400) and complexity due to multiple switching regulators. Scheme 2 (Hybrid) attempts cost reduction by using LDOs for low-current rails but fails current derating checks on the V2 rail when feeding LDOs, requiring significant redesign or component upgrades. Scheme 3 (Cost-Optimized) provides the lowest theoretical BOM cost ($6,740) but relies on aggressive derating that violates the 1.5x safety margin for V1 and V2, making it non-compliant with reliability standards. Scheme 1 is the only fully compliant and robust solution for the 85°C ambient requirement.
