Executive Summary

Scheme 1 (All-Buck) offers the highest thermal robustness and efficiency at 85°C by avoiding LDO dropout losses, but has the highest BOM cost ($36,600) and component count. Scheme 2 (Hybrid) balances cost ($18,420) and noise performance by using LDOs for sensitive low-voltage rails (V3, V7, V8) fed from Buck rails, ensuring >35dB PSRR. Scheme 3 (Cost-Optimized) provides the lowest cost ($11,800) and smallest footprint by maximizing multi-channel Buck usage (LTM4671, LTM4675) and using minimal LDOs, though it requires careful thermal management for the high-density Buck modules.
